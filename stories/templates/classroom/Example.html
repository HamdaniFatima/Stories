Introduction

This Innovation Stories application is a collaborative platform for easily creating, evaluating and consulting innovation case studies. In this application, users can sign up as a student or a expert. Students can create stories and experts can evaluate their sotries based on their interests. If you already have an account, go ahead and log in. If you are new to Innovation Stories, get started by creating a expert account or a student account.
